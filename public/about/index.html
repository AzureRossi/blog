👨‍🎓 Biography

Welcome! I am currently a second-year Master’s student in the Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science (Dept. 6) at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Xuejin Chen.
Prior to this, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology (Tencent Elite Class) from Tianjin University (TJU), where I was advised by Prof. Kun Li.

My research interests broadly lie in video generation, 3D generation, world models, and visual representation. Currently, I am a Research Intern at firstIntelligence, focusing on streaming video editing. Previously, I worked as a Research Intern at Huawei 2012 Laboratories, where my research centered on visual representation. During my time there, I also co-led and completed a project on sim2real controllable video generation under the mentorship of Prof. Xiaoxiao Long. Additionally, I am a proud member of the VGG Lab at USTC, where my academic research primarily focuses on 3D generation and 3D reconstruction.
